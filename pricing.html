Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Stealth Mode truly undetectable?

Yes. Unlike browser extensions that inject code into the page (which can be detected), Whis runs as a native OS layer. The "Ghost Overlay" uses GPU composition that screen sharing tools (Zoom, Teams) simply look right through.

What happens to my data?

We operate on a zero-retention policy. Audio is processed in RAM and discarded immediately. We do not use your data to train our models.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. You can manage your subscription directly from your account dashboard. Access remains active until the end of your billing cycle.
