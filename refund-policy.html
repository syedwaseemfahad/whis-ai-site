Cancellation and Refund Policy

Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your Whis Pro or Stealth subscription at any time via your User Profile page or by contacting support. Upon cancellation, your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period. No further charges will be applied after cancellation.

Refund Policy

Since Whis offers a digital service with immediate access to features:

  • Monthly Subscriptions: Generally non-refundable. However, if you believe you were charged in error or faced technical issues that prevented you from using the service, please contact us within 48 hours of the charge.
  • Duplicate Charges: In the event of a billing error resulting in a duplicate charge, a full refund for the duplicate amount will be processed immediately.

Processing Timeline

approved refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days and credited back to the original method of payment.
