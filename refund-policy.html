Cancellation & Refund Policy

Ensuring your satisfaction.

Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your Whis Pro or Stealth subscription at any time via your User Profile page or by contacting support. Upon cancellation:

  • Your access will remain active until the end of the current billing cycle.
  • No further charges will be applied to your payment method.
  • You will be downgraded to the Free tier automatically after the period expires.

Refund Policy

As Whis is a digital product offering immediate access to premium features, we generally do not offer refunds for partial use of a billing period. However:

  • Technical Issues: If you are unable to use the software due to a technical defect that our support team cannot resolve within 48 hours, a full refund will be issued.
  • Billing Errors: Duplicate charges or charges made after a confirmed cancellation will be refunded immediately.

Processing Timeline

Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days and credited back to the original source of payment.

Contact

For billing disputes, please email support@whis-ai.com with your Order ID.
