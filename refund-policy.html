Transparent policies for your peace of mind.

Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your subscription instantly via your User Profile. Upon cancellation:

  • Your premium access continues until the end of the current billing cycle.
  • No future charges will be applied.

Refund Eligibility

Since Whis offers immediate digital access, refunds are generally not provided for partial periods. However, we guarantee refunds in the following cases:

  • Technical Failures: If the software fails to work on your device and our support team cannot fix it within 48 hours.
  • Billing Errors: Duplicate charges or charges after a confirmed cancellation.

Processing Time

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days and credited back to the original payment method.
