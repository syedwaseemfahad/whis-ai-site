Got questions?

      


      


        

          

            
Does this work on HackerRank?

            
          

          

            

              Yes. The app runs entirely outside the browser as an isolated system process. Web-based environments like HackerRank have no way to detect or observe it operating in the background.
            

          

        


        

          

            
Is Invisible Mode truly undetectable?

            
          

          

            

              Yes. Our screen and audio capture systems bypass standard presentation layers. Zoom and Teams only broadcast standard window inputs — they cannot see a system-level process operating alongside them.
            

          

        


        

          

            
Do you offer refunds?

            
          

          

            

              Yes. If the app cannot integrate with your system within 7 days of purchase, email our support team for a full refund — no questions asked.
            

          

        


        

          

            
Which operating systems are supported?

            
          

          

            

              We natively support macOS (Intel and Apple Silicon) and Windows. Linux support is currently under development.
