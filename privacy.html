Verified Secure Architecture

Privacy by Design.

Whis-AI is built so that we cannot see or store your data, even if we wanted to. Your privacy is enforced by code, not just promises.

Regarding Windows Security Notifications:
Because Whis-AI requires high-speed access to system audio and screen data to provide real-time assistance, Microsoft Windows may flag the app as "unknown." Be assured: Whis-AI contains no trackers or malware. We prioritize your privacy above all else.

Real-Time Data Handling
Live Audio Stream NEVER SAVED

Audio is processed in Volatile RAM. This means the data exists only for a fraction of a second while the AI transcribes it, then it is permanently purged. We never write your voice to a hard drive.

Screen Context REAL-TIME ONLY

Our AI "sees" your screen only when you request assistance. We do not take screenshots, save video logs, or keep any visual history of your workspace. It is a live "pass-through" only.

Our Privacy Guarantees
  • No audio files are ever created
  • No screen recordings are stored
  • No data is used to train AI models
  • No 3rd-party data selling
  • End-to-End TLS 1.3 Encryption
  • Secure Google OAuth Login
