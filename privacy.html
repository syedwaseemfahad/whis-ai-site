Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 25, 2025

Welcome to Whis ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how your information is used. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our desktop application and services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Google User Data: We use Google OAuth for authentication. We collect your email address, name, and profile picture strictly to manage your user account.
  • Audio & Visual Data: To provide our core service, the Application processes system audio and screen captures. This data is processed in real-time to generate responses and is not stored permanently on our servers.
  • Payment Information: Payments are processed by third-party gateways (Razorpay/Cashfree). We do not store your full credit card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information strictly to:

  • Authenticate your identity and manage your subscription tier.
  • Provide the real-time transcription and AI assistance features.
  • Send transactional emails (invoices, password resets).

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL) for data in transit. Your audio streams and screenshots are processed ephemerally. We do not use your personal meeting data to train public AI models without your explicit consent.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@whis-ai.com.
