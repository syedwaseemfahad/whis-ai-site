Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Whis ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how your information is used. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our desktop application and services.

2. Information We Collect

Google User Data: We use Google OAuth for authentication. We collect your email address, name, and profile picture to create your user account.

Audio & Visual Data: To provide our core service (transcription and assistance), the Application processes system audio and screen captures locally or via our secure cloud servers. This data is transient and is processed solely for the purpose of generating real-time responses.

Payment Information: All payments are processed by third-party gateways (Razorpay/Cashfree). We do not store your full credit card details on our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Authenticate your identity.
  • Provide the transcription and AI assistance features.
  • Process subscription payments.
  • Communicate strictly regarding service updates or support.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Your audio and screen data are processed securely and are not used to train public AI models without your explicit consent.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@whis-ai.com.
