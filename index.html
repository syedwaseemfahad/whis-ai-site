Common Questions.

Does this work on HackerRank or proctored assessments?

Yes. Because the app runs as an independent, isolated desktop background process, browser-based environments like HackerRank cannot detect its presence or screen-reading activity.

Is it really undetectable on Zoom/Teams?

Yes. The tool listens to system audio and scans the screen using kernel-level technology entirely outside your browser. Meeting tools only see the shared window; they cannot detect our background process.

Can I get a refund if it doesn't work?

Absolutely. We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee. If the app doesn't perform perfectly on your machine, simply email support for a full refund. No questions asked.

Does it work on Mac and Windows?

Yes, we have native applications optimized for both Windows (10/11) and macOS (Intel & Apple Silicon M1/M2/M3).
