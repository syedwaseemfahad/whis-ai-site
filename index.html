Security isn't an afterthought.
It's the architecture.

We understand that our users discuss sensitive IP, proprietary code, and confidential financial data. Whis is engineered to be transient by default.

  • RAM-Only Processing

    Audio and image data streams are processed in volatile memory. Once the inference is complete, the buffer is zeroed out. Nothing touches the disk.

  • Encryption in Transit

    All data sent to our LLM partners is encrypted via TLS 1.3. We enforce a strict "No-Logging" policy with our AI providers.
