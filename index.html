Common Questions.

Does this work on HackerRank or proctored assessments?

Yes. Because Whis runs as an independent, isolated desktop background process, browser-based environments like HackerRank cannot detect its presence or screen-reading activity.

Is Whis really undetectable on Zoom/Teams?

Yes. Whis listens to system audio and scans the screen using technology that runs entirely outside of your browser. Meeting tools like Zoom or Teams only see the browser; they cannot detect Whis running in the background as a separate app.

Can I get a refund if it doesn't work?

Absolutely. We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee. If the app doesn't work on your laptop or you face issues we can't solve, simply email support for a full refund. No questions asked.

Does it work on Mac and Windows?

Yes, we have apps for both Windows (10/11) and macOS (Intel & M1/M2/M3 chips). Linux support is coming soon.
