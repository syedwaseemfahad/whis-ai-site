Get Started How it Works

Your Data, Strictly Private

We engineered Whis to process data transiently. What happens on your screen, stays on your screen.

Transient Audio

Audio is processed in real-time RAM buffers and discarded immediately after transcription. We never store meeting recordings.

Screenshot Analysis

Screen context is analyzed only when explicitly triggered. Images are encrypted, analyzed, and permanently deleted instantly.

Zero Retention

Our servers act as a pass-through to the LLM. We do not use your data to train models or retain logs of your conversations.

Real-time Context Awareness

Whis doesn't just transcribe. It sees your IDE, your slide deck, and your technical diagrams to give contextually accurate answers instantly.

Latency < 2s

Stealth Mode

The "Ghost Overlay" allows you to read answers while sharing your screen. Visible to you, invisible to Zoom/Teams.
